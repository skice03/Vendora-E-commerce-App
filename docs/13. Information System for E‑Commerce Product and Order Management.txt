Information System for ECommerce Product and Order Management
1. Introduction
1.1. Purpose of the document
	Specification for a system that manages products, customers, orders, and payments.
1.2. Aim of the project
The project aims to automate online shopping workflows, including product browsing, cart management, order processing, and payment tracking. The system must support product filtering, customer accounts, and sales reporting..
2. System requirements
	•	Web portal for customers and administrators.
	•	Database storing products, categories, users, carts, orders, payments, reviews.
	•	Product search with filters (price, category, rating).
	•	Shopping cart and checkout workflow.
	•	Order tracking and history.
	•	Reporting: sales, customer behavior, product performance.
	•	Data export in CSV, XML, PDF.
	•	Logging of all operations.
	•	Backup and recovery support.
